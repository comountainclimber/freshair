When are these flights?

Flights are on a fixed scheduled on a weekly basis every Thursday, Friday, Sunday and Monday.

How do I book my flights?

Members can easily book their flights in seconds and manage their reservations directly on our website. They will also be able to see flight schedules, available seats, confirmation status of flights, book guests, etc.

How often do the scheduled flight times or days change?

Scheduled flight times can change for a couple of reasons which are out of control including weather and airport operations. Occasionally, we will alter the schedule to better service our members based on these delays.

When do I receive the itinerary?

Members can expect to receive the flight itinerary up to 12 hours prior to departure. This will include the tail number, flight times, and the FBO/terminal you will be departing from.

What is the cancellation policy?

Members may cancel up to 72 hours prior to departure, anything within 72 hours is charged a cancellation fee.

Do you fly on holidays?

The typical flight schedule may change around holidays. If the regular schedule changes for an upcoming holiday, it will be posted on the online booking platform in advance.
