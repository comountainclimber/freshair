Unlimited Flights

All-you-can-fly monthly membership. Scheduled flights depart from Centennial and Broomfield Private Airports to and from Aspen, Telluride and Vail. Members can have up to two one-way flights booked at a time.



The Experience

Members can book flights in seconds, arrive 20 minutes before departure and pull right up to the plane. No lines, no wait, no hassle. Free parking and saving time by skipping the traffic make FreshAir the best way to get to the mountains.



The Aircraft

FreshAir utilizes 7-seat aircraft (turboprops or light jets) for the majority of its flights. We occasionally bump up to midsize and heavy jets during peak demand. We always fly with two seasoned mountain pilots.
