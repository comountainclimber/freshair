What does it cost to be a member?

The cost varies based on the membership type. We have Individual, Family, Business, and Corporate memberships to accommodate every flight need for you! All memberships have a flat monthly fee (please see “Memberships” tab for more information).

Can I fly as much as I want?

Members are able to book any of the flights on the schedule. There are two restrictions. The first is that a member may only have up to two (2) scheduled legs (one ways) booked at any given time. The second restriction is that a member can only complete a maximum of two (2) legs per Thursday-Monday period.

Can I try out the service before becoming a member?

Of course, we offer Introductory flights for potential new members to try our service before joining. The cost for a round trip Introductory Flight to any of our locations to and from Denver is $899 Round trip ($449.50 one way). Each person is able to take one (1) round trip introductory flight or two (2) single legs during a calendar year.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment period?

FreshAir has a non-binding, subscription based membership agreement. There is no contract to become a FreshAir member and a 3 month minimum commitment period for any new member. If you have a request for a custom membership please reach out to us directly.

What are the policies on Cancellations/ freezing a membership?

A member can freeze or cancel their membership with 30 days written notice at any time. A member can freeze their account for up to 6 months at a time and can only freeze their account once in a calendar year.
